Setup Instructions

To install your theme, go to your VSCode direcotry (C:/users/{username}/.vscode/extensions). In the extensions folder, create a folder called the name of your theme and paste the two files that were just downloaded into your newly-created folder. Restart VSCode and switch to your theme by pressing Ctrl-K + Ctrl-T.
